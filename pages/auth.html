Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026  ·  Last Updated: June 1, 2026

ARTX ("we", "our", or "us") operates the ARTX platform, a competitive creative community where artists earn prestige, build streaks, form alliances, and compete for real cash prizes. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal information when you use our website, mobile experience, and related services (collectively, the "Platform").

By registering or using the Platform you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Platform.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account data — username, email address, and password when you register.
  • Profile data — display name, bio, profile picture, and any social media handles you choose to link.
  • Payment & payout data — billing information and payout details required to process competition winnings. Payment card data is handled exclusively by our PCI-compliant payment processor and is never stored on our servers.
  • User-generated content — artwork, submissions, comments, and messages you post on the Platform.
  • Communications — support tickets, feedback, and any other messages you send us.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage data — pages visited, features used, challenges entered, and interaction timestamps.
  • Device & browser data — IP address, browser type and version, operating system, and device identifiers.
  • Cookies & similar technologies — session cookies to keep you signed in, preference cookies to remember your settings, and analytics cookies to understand how the Platform is used.

1.3 Information from Third Parties

If you connect a social media account (Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, etc.) we receive basic profile information and follower count from that platform to populate your ARTX profile and determine verification status. We only access the permissions you explicitly grant.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Process competition entries, calculate rankings, and disburse prize money.
  • Display your profile, artwork, and stats to other community members.
  • Send transactional emails (account confirmation, password reset, prize notifications).
  • Send promotional communications — you may opt out at any time via the unsubscribe link in any email.
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and Terms of Service violations.
  • Analyse usage trends to improve the Platform.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it only in the following circumstances:

  • Service providers — third-party vendors who help us operate the Platform (hosting, email delivery, payment processing, analytics). They are contractually required to protect your data and may not use it for their own purposes.
  • Other users — your username, profile picture, artwork submissions, prestige level, and ranking are visible to other ARTX members as part of the competitive experience.
  • Legal requirements — when required by law, court order, or to protect the rights, safety, or property of ARTX or others.
  • Business transfers — in the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you before your data is subject to a different privacy policy.

4. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account we will remove your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal, tax, or financial record-keeping purposes (typically up to 7 years for transaction records).

5. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction you may have the right to:

  • Access a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.
  • Data portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@artxplatform.com. We will respond within 30 days.

6. Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect Platform functionality. We do not currently respond to "Do Not Track" browser signals, but we support the Global Privacy Control (GPC) signal where applicable.

7. Security

We use industry-standard safeguards including TLS encryption in transit, hashed passwords (bcrypt), and access controls. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. Please use a strong, unique password and enable two-factor authentication when available.

8. Children's Privacy

ARTX is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe a minor has created an account, contact us at privacy@artxplatform.com and we will promptly delete the account.

9. International Transfers

ARTX is operated from and your data may be processed in countries outside your own. Where required, we rely on approved transfer mechanisms (such as Standard Contractual Clauses) to protect your data.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by email or by posting a prominent notice on the Platform at least 14 days before the change takes effect. Your continued use after the effective date constitutes acceptance.

About ARTX

Where Skill Creates Prestige

ARTX is a competitive creative platform built for artists who want more than likes — they want recognition, community, and real rewards for their craft.

Our Story

ARTX was born from a simple frustration: the internet is full of talented artists who never get their moment. Social media rewards follower counts over skill, and most creative competitions are either pay-to-play or hidden behind gatekeepers.

We built ARTX to flip that. A place where the work speaks for itself — where a newcomer with raw talent can go head-to-head with a seasoned pro, earn community recognition, and take home real prize money based purely on their art.

What We Do

Challenges

Weekly and monthly creative challenges across disciplines — illustration, photography, design, digital art, and more. Each one with a clear brief, a prize pool, and a deadline.

Prestige System

Earn prestige points for every submission, win, and streak. Your rank reflects your consistency and quality — not your follower count.

Streaks

Show up every day. Build your streak. The longer you stay consistent, the bigger the multipliers on your prestige earnings.

Alliances

Form crews with other artists. Alliance members compete together in team events, share knowledge, and keep each other accountable.

Real Prizes

Cash payouts, not gift cards or exposure. Top finishers in every challenge earn real money, paid out directly to their account within 14 business days.

Fair Competition

Transparent judging. Community voting with anti-manipulation safeguards. Every submission gets a fair shot.

Our Values

  • Skill first. Rankings and rewards are based on the quality of your work, nothing else.
  • Community over clout. We're building a space where artists support each other, not just compete.
  • Radical transparency. Prize pools, judging criteria, and platform rules are always visible upfront.
  • Accessibility. Free to join, free to compete. No premium tiers that lock you out of prizes.
  • Original work. We celebrate human creativity and protect the integrity of every competition.

Who It's For

ARTX is for any artist 18 or older who wants to:

  • Push their creative limits with structured challenges
  • Build a portfolio of competitive work
  • Earn recognition beyond social media vanity metrics
  • Connect with a serious, motivated creative community
  • Win money doing what they love

Join Us

ARTX is growing fast. Thousands of artists are already competing, building streaks, and forming alliances. Whether you're a hobbyist who wants to take their work seriously or a professional looking for a new competitive outlet — there's a place for you here.

Create your account, enter your first challenge, and find out where your skill stands.
